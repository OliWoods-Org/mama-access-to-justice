> **80% of civil legal needs** of low-income Americans go unmet. Tenants don't know their rights. Consumers don't know how to fight back. The justice gap is a feature of the system, not a bug.

## The Solution

5 agents providing free legal information (not advice) to level the playing field.

## Agents

**TenantRights** — State-specific tenant rights with statute citations and template letters
**SmallClaimsGuide** — Should you sue? Step-by-step guide, forms, court locations
**ConsumerProtection** — Scam reporting, FTC complaints, state AG contacts
**LegalAidFinder** — Free/low-cost legal aid by location and income
**KnowYourRights** — Police encounters, workplace rights, basic immigration info

## Safety

**MANDATORY disclaimer:** This is general legal information, NOT legal advice. For advice specific to your situation, consult a licensed attorney.

## Data Sources

Legal Services Corporation directory, state bar associations, NLIHC tenant database, Cornell LII
